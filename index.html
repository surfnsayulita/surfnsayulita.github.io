Sayulita Lessons

Our qualified instructors do both private and group lessons for people of all ages.

Read More
$1350 pesos for a private lesson.
$1050 pesos/pax for group lesson (2+ pax)

Surf lesson includes:

Rash guard (UV protection) Surfboard Leash.
We start with a 20-30min instruction in the sand where we explain all the basics and practice popping up on the board.
That’s when we go in the water with an ISA certified surf instructor for 1.5 hrs where we make sure your catching waves and having lots of fun. After we are done with the surf lesson you can use the surfboard for a free hr so you can practice what you’ve learn.

Surf Trips

We make day trips to some of the best and secluded surf spots

Read More
$1750 pesos w/ surf lesson.
$1400 pesos a surfer without lesson.

Surf trip includes:

Transportation, ice cooler with waters, board and rashguard.
We leave town at 9:30 a.m. We drive for 25-35 min. We hike though the jungle for another 6-8 min, we arrive to white sand, blue water, nice waves where we stay for 2.5 hrs approx then we go to a town in between where we enjoy good lunch (fish-shrimp tacos) and a cold drink arriving back to town around 1:30 p.m.
